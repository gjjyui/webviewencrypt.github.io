Alarm Email Distribution worksheet

View, create, and modify alarm email recipients for entire Enterprise Websites using the Alarm Email Distribution worksheet.

To select the alarm information to include in an email subject line and body, select or clear the appropriate check boxes in the Alarm Emails area of the Enterprise Website Settings page.

To access the Alarm Email Distribution worksheet, click an Enterprise Website in the System Tree, then click Configure Distribution in the Alarm Emails area on the Enterprise Website Settings page.

The Alarm Email Distribution worksheet provides the following functionality:

Filters

Users

Filter the email distribution recipients by users.

Systems

Filter the email distribution recipients by systems.

Toolbar buttons

Add an email alarm recipient to the worksheet.
Delete an email alarm recipient from the worksheet.

Worksheet columns

Alarm Types

Select alarm types for notifications.

Days

Select one or multiple days of the week to receive alarms. The days correspond to when the alarm event was initiated.

Systems

Click the ellipsis (...) to open the Selected Systems dialog box. Select one or multiple systems, and RC-WebView sends alarm emails based on alarms in these systems. Email notifications are automatically filtered out for users who do not have access to the associated system, regardless of the configuration in the worksheet.

Recipients

Add alarm email recipients by email address, username, or user role. Click the ellipsis (...) to open the Selected Recipients dialog box. Once you have selected recipients, click the Add icon in the Selected Recipients dialog box to add them to the email recipient group. To remove a recipient from the email recipient group, click the ellipsis to open the Selected Recipients dialog box, select a user, and click the Delete icon. If a recipient cannot receive an email, their username appears in red in the Recipients column. Click the Close icon next to the username to remove the recipient from the column.

  • Email — Enter an email address to receive alarm notification emails.
  • User — Select a user to receive alarm notification emails. If a username is red in the Selected Recipients dialog box, there is no email address configured for this user or they do not have access to any of the selected systems. Hover over the username and click the link to update the user accordingly.
  • Role — Select a role to receive alarm notification emails. All users that inherit this role receive the email if they have an email address configured. If a user role is red in the Selected Recipients dialog box, they do not have access to any of the systems designated for this email group.

HTML

Enable or disable HTML formatting for the alarm emails. If HTML formatting is disabled, the alarm emails contain only plain text. Emails with HTML formatting also contain a RC-WebView logon link.
